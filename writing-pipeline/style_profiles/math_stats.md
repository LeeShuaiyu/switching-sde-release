# Style Profile: Math or Statistics

## Use when

The target values formal definitions, assumptions, notation discipline, and precise interpretation over broad rhetorical framing.

## Writing traits

- precise and restrained
- careful distinction between assumptions, definitions, propositions, and empirical findings
- explicit notation management
- moderate paragraph length with high information density

## Emphasis

- formal problem statement
- notation consistency
- assumptions behind the method or experiment
- interpretation that stays within what the analysis supports

## Avoid

- informal leaps from empirical trend to general theorem-like claim
- notation drift
- vague qualitative adjectives

## Section rhythm

- define objects clearly
- state assumptions before consequences
- present empirical evidence as support, not as a substitute for formal argument
