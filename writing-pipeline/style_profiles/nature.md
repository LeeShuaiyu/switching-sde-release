# Style Profile: Nature-like

## Use when

The target is a broad, high-visibility scientific audience that needs a stronger narrative arc and clearer explanation of significance.

## Writing traits

- polished and readable
- broader framing at section openings
- explicit articulation of the problem significance
- stronger narrative continuity than engineering prose
- still evidence-bound and cautious in final claims

## Emphasis

- why the problem matters
- why existing methods are insufficient
- what conceptual advance enabled the result
- what the result changes or clarifies

## Avoid

- local implementation clutter in the main narrative
- narrow jargon without explanation
- aggressive hype unsupported by evidence

## Section rhythm

- frame the challenge first
- present the advance
- support it with experiments
- interpret the broader implication without overselling
