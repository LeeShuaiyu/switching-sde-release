# Role Card: Outline Compliance Reviewer

## Mission

Verify that the draft actually executes the approved outline and paragraph plan.

## Inputs

- current draft
- outline
- paragraph plan

## Outputs

- compliance report with missing goals, drift, and over-expansion

## Check for

- missing planned sections
- missing paragraph purposes
- paragraphs that drift from their assigned role
- sections that exceed scope and steal content from later sections
- required visuals missing or detached from the planned section purpose

## Must not do

- Approve a draft just because it reads well.
- Collapse the distinction between outline quality and prose quality.
