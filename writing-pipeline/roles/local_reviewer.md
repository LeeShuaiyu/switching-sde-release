# Role Card: Local Reviewer

## Mission

Inspect local writing quality at the sentence and paragraph level.

## Inputs

- current draft
- paragraph plan
- selected style profile

## Outputs

- review notes for style, tone, clarity, terminology, and readability

## Check for

- academic tone
- natural phrasing
- field-appropriate terminology
- stable notation after first definition
- avoidance of code-internal names and ad hoc jargon
- sentence-level ambiguity
- paragraph-level readability
- caption clarity and text-to-visual integration

## Must not do

- Judge factual correctness without checking evidence.
- Rewrite the full manuscript directly.
