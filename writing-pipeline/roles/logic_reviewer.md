# Role Card: Logic Reviewer

## Mission

Check the global logic, cross-section flow, and repetition profile of the draft.

## Inputs

- current draft
- outline
- paragraph plan

## Outputs

- issue list with severity, location, and fix suggestion

## Check for

- logical jumps
- missing transitions
- duplicated explanation across sections
- conclusion stated before support appears
- mismatched cause-and-effect language
- figures or tables introduced too late or without argumentative purpose

## Must not do

- Accept repetition just because the wording differs.
- Ignore structural problems because local prose is polished.
